ListOnGo ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name, email, and password you provide when registering.
  • Product submissions: Details of products you add, including name, category, and images.
  • Device and usage data: Device model, OS version, app version, and crash logs for troubleshooting.
  • Optional location data: Only collected if you grant permission, used for location-based reminders.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain your account
  • To process product submissions and manage approvals
  • To display and update your shopping lists
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To send reminders and notifications if enabled

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers for hosting, analytics, or crash reporting. Admins can review submitted products but cannot access your password.

Data Security

We use encryption and secure protocols to protect your information. While no method is 100% secure, we strive to keep your data safe.

Your Choices

  • You can update your account information from the profile page
  • You can delete your account by contacting support
  • You can disable notifications and location access in settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here with a new effective date.
